Tips to use:

    To find alternate forms such as mega evolutions, suffix your pokemon name with "-form":

  • charizard-mega-x
  • vulpix-alola
  • meowth-gmax

    • Pokemon have had special characters removed and are dash seperated as followed:

  • Nidoran-f and nidoran-m are called accordingly
  • Type: Null may be found via type-null
  • Galarian Mr. Mime may be found via Mr-mime-galar

    • Have fun!
